About Me

Racing driver for Red Bull Racing

At the 2015 Australian Grand Prix, when I was aged 17 years, 166 days, I became the youngest driver to compete in Formula One. I was granted the super licence and raced in Formula One before being able to legally drive a road car in my native Netherlands. At the age of 18, I won the 2016 Spanish Grand Prix on my debut for Red Bull Racing, becoming the youngest-ever driver and the first Dutch driver to win a Formula One Grand Prix. After winning the 2021 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, I became the first Dutch driver to win the Formula One World Championship, and the 34th Formula One World Drivers' Champion.
